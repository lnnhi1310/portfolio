Personal Project

⊹ ࣪ ˖DNA. SKRT

Description

THis is an innovative 3D model design project that embodies a street-style aesthetic with a bold metallic theme. The centerpiece features a meticulously sculpted metallic skull adorned with intricate engravings of detail. The animation enhances the visual narrative, incorporating falling coins and ethereal smoke emanating from the eye sockets, creating a dynamic and immersive atmosphere. This project represents a seamless fusion of digital craftsmanship and urban modernity, delivering a compelling and visually striking experience.

Manipulation

⊹ ࣪ ˖from the fantasy

Description

"The Reading Nook" is my first 3D artwork, created during the Digital Media Studio 1 course. Inspired by the cottagecore aesthetic and European Middle Ages, it blends rustic charm with historical ambiance. The room, featuring warm earthy tones and detailed textures, is enhanced through Photoshop manipulation to achieve a realistic look. Elements like the glowing stone oven and the serene figure reading by the light-filled archway evoke a tranquil moment that merges nature and human craft.

School Project

⊹ ࣪ ˖The Lost Labyrinth

Description

Inspired by "The Raft of the Medusa" painting, this project captures an ethereal scene within a forgotten labyrinth. It features a serene yet haunting environment, where a stone figure adorned with vines stands amidst misty waters and skeletal remains. The imagery conveys themes of mystery, decay, and the passage of time, inviting viewers into a world of forgotten stories and silent beauty.

School Project

⊹ ࣪ ˖ILLUSORY CIRCUS

Description

Bá Đạt is a 3D character inspired by Northern Vietnamese farmers in 1945. His frail build, scars, and scorched legs reflect the struggles of famine and colonial exploitation. Despite societal rejection due to his appearance, Bá Đạt’s brave, generous spirit shines as he defends his homeland. Designed with Blender and detailed texturing, he embodies an antihero’s resilience, earning the title "Eastern Pearl Killer."
