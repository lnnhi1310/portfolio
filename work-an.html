Description

In this ambitious project, our goal is to craft a captivating short 3D animation that vividly depicts the diverse tapestry of Vietnamese history, art, and cultural across three distinct eras. We plan to employ 3D printing technology to provide audiences with a truly immersive experience, allowing them to not only witness but also collect intricate 3D figures inspired by Vietnamese culture, turning this endeavor into both an educational journey and a deligthful collecting experience.

Description

Illusory Circus is our Projection Mapping experiment through a Visual Jockey (VJ) project. We paired captivating visual assets with the music "Lazarski Dream", "Gardarike" by Tri-Tachyon, "Racing Energetic Electronic | Racing" by Alex Productions, to create a VJ live session. Our project tells a story about teddy bunnies kidnapped by a witch and trapped in a "fake circus." Hypnotized for a spiritual ritual, the bunnies eventually awaken and attempt to escape but are ultimately caught. The music's mysterious and spiritual vibe, combined with energetic electronic elements, enhances the thrilling experience of our performance.
